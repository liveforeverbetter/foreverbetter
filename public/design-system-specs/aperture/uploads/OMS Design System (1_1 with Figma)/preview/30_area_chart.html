Area chart

Area charts visualize trends over time, making it easy to understand changes in volume or performance at a glance. They are useful for highlighting growth, patterns, and overall direction. Use area charts for continuous data such as transactions, revenue, or activity over time. Include a clear label and key metric for context, and ensure axes are easy to read. When no data is available, show a clear empty state within the chart to communicate that data has not yet been populated.
