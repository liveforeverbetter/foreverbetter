Status banner

Status banners communicate system states and guide users toward the next step. Use success for completed actions, info for neutral updates, warning for situations that require attention, and error for issues that block progress. Choose the status based on urgency and impact: warnings highlight incomplete or required actions (e.g. pending KYC), while errors indicate something has failed or must be resolved. Pair banners with clear, concise messaging and include a relevant action when possible (e.g. "Start KYC" or "See details") to help users move forward.
