Tooltip

Tooltips provide additional context or guidance when users hover over or focus on an element. They help clarify functionality without adding permanent UI clutter. Use tooltips for brief, helpful information such as explanations of icons or actions. Keep content concise and avoid using them for critical information, as they may not always be visible on all devices.
