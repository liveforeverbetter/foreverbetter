Empty state

Empty states communicate that no data or results are available and guide users on what to do next. They help prevent confusion by clearly explaining why a section is empty. Use empty states when a table, list, or view has no content. Include a short, clear message and provide a primary action (and optional secondary action) to help users move forward, such as creating, adding, or importing data. Keep the tone informative and supportive, not alarming.
