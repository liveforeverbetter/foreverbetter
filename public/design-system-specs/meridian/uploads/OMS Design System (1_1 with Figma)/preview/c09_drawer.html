Drawer

A drawer is a fixed-position panel that slides in from the edge of the screen over a blurred overlay, allowing users to access supplementary content without leaving the current view. It includes a persistent header with a title and close action, a scrollable body for detailed or structured information, and an optional fixed footer for primary and secondary CTAs. The content areas are flexible, with editable slots for primary and secondary information and optional dividers to organize sections, making it suitable for reviewing details, managing data, or completing lightweight tasks.
