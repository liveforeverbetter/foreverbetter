Scroll area

Scroll areas allow users to navigate overflow content within a fixed container. They maintain layout structure while enabling access to additional content horizontally or vertically. Use scroll areas when content exceeds available space, such as in tables, panels, or sidebars. Ensure scroll behavior is smooth and visible, and avoid nesting multiple scroll areas to prevent usability issues.
