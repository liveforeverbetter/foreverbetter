Verification input

Verification inputs capture short, fixed-length codes such as one-time passwords (OTP), breaking entry into individual fields to improve readability and reduce errors. Use for secure code-entry flows. Auto-advance focus between fields, support pasting a full code, and surface active, filled, error, and disabled states clearly.
