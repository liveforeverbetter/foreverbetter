Cards

Cards group related information and actions into a single, digestible container. They help organize content and make it easier to scan and interact with. Use cards to highlight key resources, features, or navigation paths. Keep content concise, with a clear title, optional supporting text, and a single primary action when needed. Ensure hover and tap states provide subtle feedback to indicate interactivity.
