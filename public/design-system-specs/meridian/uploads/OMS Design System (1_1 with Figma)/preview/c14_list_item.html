List item

List items represent individual entries within a list — users, payment methods, selectable options. They provide a consistent way to display key information alongside relevant metadata and actions. Use list items for scannable collections where users review or act on multiple entries. Support hover, selected, and status variants (e.g. active vs pending) to reflect state.
